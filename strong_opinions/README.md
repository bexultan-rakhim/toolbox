Strong Opinions
===============
What
----

This is a folder of my strong opinions. I update them when I see resolution if I was right or wrong.


Why
---

Having strong opinion and being confident in them is a way of learning. If you are an engineer having a stake at some important role, you should voice confidence, opinions that go long way, and learn from outcomes. Last part is the most important. Learning also means to have a place where you can flesh out this opinion and fix it without ability to move the goalpost later. And of course, accept the outcomes either it aligns with your opinion or not.


How
---

Different small or big opinion with dated updates and commentaries on how it changes as time passes.
